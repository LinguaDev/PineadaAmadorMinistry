Support the Ministry

Both my wife Anita and I work as English teachers, which helps sustain our family day-to-day. At the same time, we are deeply committed to serving the Lord full-time. I am currently raising support to pursue further studies in theology so I can serve the Kingdom with greater depth and faithfulness. We would greatly appreciate more help to continue this calling: Anita focuses especially on teaching and nurturing children in the faith, while I dedicate time to writing Gospel-centered content and building online resources to preach Christ across the world through the internet.

Long-term vision: I dream of helping establish a Christian university platform — beginning online — across Latin America. The goal is to offer high-quality theological training and professional degrees either free or at very low cost, especially for preachers and Christian workers. Following the example of the Apostle Paul, who made tents to support himself while preaching (Acts 18:3), we believe God equips His servants with both spiritual wisdom and practical professions. This way, ministers can preach boldly without financial pressure and serve their communities sustainably for many years to come.

Your gifts — whether through Western Union for direct family & ministry needs or PayPal for digital outreach — make this vision possible. Thank you for standing with us in prayer and generosity.
