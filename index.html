How Shall They Hear?

"How then shall they call on Him in whom they have not believed? And how shall they believe in Him of whom they have not heard? And how shall they hear without a preacher? And how shall they preach unless they are sent? As it is written: 'How beautiful are the feet of those who preach the gospel of peace, who bring glad tidings of good things!'"
— Romans 10:14-15

Every mission requires a sender. Your support provides the means for the Gospel to reach those who have not yet heard. Join us in this ministry to ensure the Word of God continues to be taught and shared.
